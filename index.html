ZBB Fundamentals
🐷 The Piggy Bank Philosophy
Zero-based budgeting means every dollar you have gets a job. You can't spend money you haven't assigned, and you can't assign money you don't have in the bank. This app enforces that — the "Ready to Assign" number is your truth.
✉️ Envelopes, Not Categories
Each envelope holds real money. When you spend from Groceries, that envelope shrinks. When it hits zero, you're done — unless you move money from another envelope. This is the discipline that makes ZBB work.
💳 Credit Cards Are Not Cash
When you charge something to a credit card, mark it as "credit." The expense still drains your envelope — because you WILL pay that bill — but it's tracked separately. The goal is to always have the money sitting in your account before the bill comes.
🔁 Reimbursements Don't Count as Income
If you paid for something and someone pays you back, that money isn't new income — it goes straight back to the envelope it came from. Toggle "Reimbursement" when logging it so your budget stays honest.
📅 Weekly Thinkers
If you get paid weekly, enable Weekly Mode in Settings. The budget view will show per-paycheck target amounts for each envelope (monthly amount ÷ 4), so your mental model matches your actual cash flow.
🏦 Aging Your Money
The ultimate ZBB goal: next month's expenses are funded by this month's income. When you reach this, you're budgeting 30 days ahead and financial stress drops dramatically. Work toward it by letting your "Ready to Assign" sit unspent at month end.
